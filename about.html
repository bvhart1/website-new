Benjamin Hart

Mr. Benjamin Hart is one of the band directors at Eau Gallie High School. His duties include, teaching the Symphonic band and both Jazz bands. Mr. Hart also spends time supporting the Johnson Middle School Band. Prior to his appointment at Eau Gallie High School, He was the band director at Tavares Middle School in Tavares, Florida. His duties at Tavares included teaching both beginning and advanced concert and Jazz bands. He also taught one of the three Jazz bands at Tavares High School.

Mr. Hart graduated with a Bachelors in Music Education from the Florida State University, where he studied conducting with Professor Richard Clary, music education with Dr. Steve Kelly, and interned with Mr. John Nista at Ramblewood Middle School. During his time at Florida State, he performed with the Wind Orchestra, Symphonic Band and Concert bands, as well as various jazz combos, chamber ensembles and the Florida State University Marching Chiefs. Mr. Hart has received various awards from the College of Music, including the Colborn Family Marching Chiefs Scholarship, and the William L. Hughes Top Music Educator award. His professional affiliations include Phi Mu Alpha Sinfonia, Florida Bandmasters Association, the American String Teacher Association, Florida Music Educators Association and the National Association for Music Education.
